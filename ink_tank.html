Ink Tank

Ink Tank is a responsive web app that provides information and inspiration from wide range of Tattoos designs and artists, specializing in different styles. As you can imagine, choosing a permanent tattoo design is an incredible personal experience but it can be quite stressful and complicated. Therefore, Ink Tank app offers a step by step guide and services for Tattoo enthusiast and Tattoo seekers to help with their Tattoo Planning. This is the story of how I  designed the app that aims to deliver a pleasant experience during the Tattoo planning and make people feel confident with their Tattoo decision at the end, with no regrets.

To comply with my confidentiality agreement I have omitted and appropriated confidential information.
